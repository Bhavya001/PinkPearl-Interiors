Services

We do turnkey interior projects, our work includes all types of interior decoration works. We do contracting works of commercial, residential, showrooms, shops, hotels, restaurant, clinic, boutique, beauty parlour, entrance lobby and many more.

Turnkey Contract (Design & Build)

Turnkey project or design-n-build service under this type, pink pearl interiors undertakes complete responsibility for interior design services and execution till handover. At pink pearl interiors, turnkey contract, the client gets the hassle free project.The client has to do is finalise the material which we will be proposed from our expert team which assure the quality of materials like plywood, wood, hardware, type of plumbing, accessories and finishing items like veneer, laminate, marble, tiles, light fittings, curtains etc. with best option and you get immense benefits in this kind of contract with single window responsibility for design, execution and professional & trained team to look after your project.

Interior Design Consultancy

For those clients who have their own team of vendors and contractors. pink pearl interiors provides the client with complete detailed interior design along with site supervision for quality and design assurance. our scope of work for this kind of service includes but not limited :
   Making initial interior design based on clients requirements.
  On finalizing interior design layout pink pearl interiors undertakes making detailed   designs and drawings of interior ie! ceiling, flooring and furniture, electrical,       plumbing and tiling works.
   Finalizing finishing materials for the interiors.
  Compiling a document of drawings and interior design references.
  Making a BOQ for inviting quotations to arrive at interior project cost.
  Regular site supervision for interior design and quality assurance.
  Explaining interior designs to the contractor.
